Prontos Parachutes

Adds a functional T-10 static-line parachute for realistic airborne jumps and MilSim missions.

Developed for the Bushwar Community and general military simulation use, this mod adds
T-10 static-line parachutes to the United States arsenal box. Gear up, jump, ride the
canopy down, and mind your landing - come in too hard and you'll pay for it.

STANDALONE - REPLACES THE FRAMEWORK
No longer requires the Parachute Framework. Everything needed is bundled, so server
owners only subscribe to this one mod. Do NOT run it alongside the Parachute
Framework - both patch the same base-game player controller and will conflict. This
mod replaces the framework; it is not a companion to it.

FEATURES
- T-10 static-line parachute in the US arsenal box
- Steerable canopy: pitch, roll, and bank-to-turn, with a landing flare
- Safe-landing check - injures or kills above a configurable vertical speed
- Damageable canopy that can collapse and force an emergency exit
- Built and tested for multiplayer
- Fixes the multiplayer bug that could send players back to the spawn screen on landing

HOW TO USE
Just enable the mod and add the T-10 parachute bag to your loadout / arsenal. No
controller setup needed - the parachute is injected into every player automatically.
