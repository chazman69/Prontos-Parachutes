Prontos Parachutes

Adds a functional T-10 static-line parachute for realistic airborne jumps and MilSim missions.

Developed for the Bushwar Community and general military simulation use, this mod adds
T-10 static-line parachutes to the United States arsenal box. Gear up, jump, ride the
canopy down, and mind your landing - come in too hard and you'll pay for it.

Built on the Parachute Framework by Alphaegen, with a multiplayer landing fix on top:
players no longer get sent back to the spawn screen when they touch down.

REQUIRED DEPENDENCY
- Parachute Framework (by Alphaegen)

FEATURES
- T-10 static-line parachute in the US arsenal box
- Steerable canopy with landing flare and safe-landing checks (from the framework)
- Fixes the multiplayer bug that could delete a player on landing and return them
  to the spawn screen

CREDITS
Built on the Arma Reforger Parachute Framework by Alphaegen - thank you.
https://github.com/Alphaegen/ArmaReforgerParachutes
This mod keeps the framework as a dependency and only patches the landing behaviour
through script overrides.
